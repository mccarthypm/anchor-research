During 2012 and
2011, the Company completed various business acquisitions. In 2012,
the aggregate cash consideration, net of cash acquired, was $350
million, of which $245 million was allocated to goodwill,
$113 million to acquired intangible assets and $8 million to
liabilities assumed. In 2011, the aggregate cash consideration, net
of cash acquired, was $244 million, of which $167 million was
allocated to goodwill and $77 million to acquired intangible
assets.

The
Company’s gross carrying amount of goodwill was $1.1 billion
and $896 million as of September 29, 2012 and
September 24, 2011, respectively. The Company did not have any
goodwill impairment during 2012, 2011 or 2010.
