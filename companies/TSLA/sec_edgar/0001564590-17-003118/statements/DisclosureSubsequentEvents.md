Acquisition of Grohmann Engineering GmbH

On January 3, 2017, we completed the acquisition of Grohmann Engineering GmbH 
for approximately $150 million in cash. Management is currently determining the 
fair value of assets acquired and liabilities assumed necessary to evaluate the 
purchase price allocation for this transaction.

New Debt Facility

On January 27, 2017, a subsidiary of the Company issued $145.0 million in 
aggregate principal of solar loan-backed notes with a final maturity date of 
September 2049. The solar loan-backed notes are secured by certain customer 
loans under the MyPower program.
